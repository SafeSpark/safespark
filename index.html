Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our clients and partners.

Do you supply to cities outside Meerut?
Yes. SafeSpark has a pan India supply network. We regularly ship materials to Delhi, Noida, Ghaziabad, Lucknow, Mumbai, Bangalore and other major cities. Freight is co-ordinated and delivery is tracked.
What is the minimum order quantity for bulk supply?
There is no fixed minimum. We cater to both small orders (10–20 units for a single apartment) and large project orders (10,000+ units). Pricing and delivery terms are customised based on order size.
Are your products genuine and under warranty?
Absolutely. We source all products from authorised brand channels. Every product carries the manufacturer's standard warranty and is BIS compliant. Bills and warranty cards are provided with every shipment.
Can you supply for government tenders?
Yes. We have experience supplying for government school electrification, municipal lighting and infrastructure projects. We provide all necessary documentation including GST invoices, test certificates and brand authorisation letters.
How long does it take to get a quotation?
We aim to send a detailed quotation within 24 hours of receiving your requirement. For urgent requirements, WhatsApp us directly at 9997866880 and we can often respond within a few hours.
Do you offer credit / payment terms for contractors?
We offer flexible payment terms for established contractors and builders with a track record. Credit limits and terms are determined on a case-by-case basis after an initial discussion with our accounts team.
